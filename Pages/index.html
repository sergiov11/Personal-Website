Tracking the Journey: LOTR Checkouts at Seattle Public Librarye | R, HTML

Project Link

For this project, I chose to analyze data from the Seattle Public Library related to J.R.R. Tolkien and his popular book series, "The Lord of the Rings." This analysis provided fascinating insights into the borrowing patterns of these beloved books. I discovered that "The Lord of the Rings: The Fellowship of the Ring" had the most checkouts at the Seattle Public Library, with audiobooks being the most commonly borrowed format. Additionally, 2022 was the year with the highest number of checkouts, averaging 47.87 books per month. An interesting random fact I uncovered was that 2023 had the fewest eBook checkouts. Overall, this analysis highlights intriguing trends in the popularity of Tolkien's work within the Seattle community.
